From a young age I have always drawn by hand, and having studied illustration at London Metropolitan University I decided to opt for the original form and meaning of word illustration. Here I have been able to use these skills, and with the progress in my Adobe Illustrator skills, this has allowed me to produce illustrated images and line drawings. Featured below are a small selection of my original illustrations as well as line drawings that have been done digitally in Adobe Illustrator.

Travelling People

Studying the different people (apart from the Queen who was in the Metro Newspaper) on the tube as I made my way to London Metropolitan University. This gave me something to do, practice my skills and experiment with different techniques. This also allowed me to use these characters in my illustrated story that we made as part of the illustration module.
Created using: Fineliner or Biro pen.

Hand Drawn Illustrations

The illustrations that I have completed above are again as part of practicing my hand drawn illustration skills. I decided to look at Games Workshop and Disney characters and draw the Crocodile, Jester and River Troll. I practiced using pencil to caricature my college tutor, although didn't get the mouth right so have yet to finish this. My cow illustration is based on my Bronze Expedition that I completed as part of the Duke of Edinburgh's Award. I expanded this as a painting during after school Art Club and the teachers liked it so much they put it in the staff room as they liked it and it cheered them up.
Created using: Pencil(s) or Biro pen.

Fish Illustrations

Practicing with Adobe Illustrator I decided to create some fish as they are quite simple and easy to do. I thought I would use varying colours as a gradient on all of the fish. The gradient helps to show a bit of contrast and some shadow too. I decided to stretch out the original shape of the larger orange fish in order to and compress this down to form the other fish that are on the page. With a change in colour gradients this made them differ slightly from the others that are there.
Created in: Adobe Illustrator.

The Glassman

I was asked by a glass repairer if I could use the Glassman font and image from Google but make this high res for their business. I took the black and white image and traced round this, then added the effects to make this look more like a shard of glass. The glass repairer liked all of three of the images, but decided the first image was their favourite as it looked more glass like.
Created in: Adobe Illustrator.
Image traced was originally from: Google.
